Ever had the frustrating experience of not being able to decide where to eat? When Yelp gives you too many choices that you get mired down in the decision-making, Noms is here to help!

Noms is an iOS restaurant discovery app that removes the hassle of decision making and never let you worry about restaurant selection again.
